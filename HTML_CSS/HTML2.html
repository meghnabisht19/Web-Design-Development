    • Prof. S. P. Aggarwal, Principal, Ramanujan College
    Greetings from Ramanujan College.
    Ramanujan College is an institution that is now well known for its commitment towards excellence in every aspect of its institutional life. The college has grown and evolved to become one of the most esteemed and prestigious college of the University of Delhi. In the past few years we have added many new courses and expanded exponentially in infrastructure development, providing for our students higher level teaching-learning experience, more extra-curriculum activities, add-on courses, outreach programs and opportunities for placement. In the coming years the College will extensively focus on the National Education Policy mandated blended learning programmes, skill based education, capacity building of its teachers and students and the non-teaching staff and focus on further strengthening the Information Technology environment of the institution. The spirit of our college resonates with all its staff and students. I hope all of you will spend enriching and the most memorable years of your life in our college and make bonds lasting a lifetime
    Best Wishes,
    Prof. S.P. Aggarwal
